What Is Deaconn?

Deaconn is Christian Deacon's website and portfolio.

I am a self-taught technical engineer interested in programming/modding, networking, and general technical engineering. I enjoy learning, progressing/improving, and working within many areas in the technology industry. This ranges from game modding/programming, network programming (e.g. (D)DoS protection/mitigation), pen-testing, web development/general tools, and AI/robotic engineering (includes machine learning) to giving suggestions/feedback on how to improve tools/mods. I really enjoy writing and constructing plans as well!
