Who Are We?

We are a community that shines spotlight and support onto the modding community. We are trying to make modding the norm! We are introducing as many people to the modding community which in return will improve content creation, gaming, career opportunities, talent, and more!

This is a MAJOR project and has A LOT of potential to change the world with our plans, experience, and ambition! We already have a great team including a game company pushing everything forward and that is only going to expand.


If you want to be a part of a community where you can interact with other talented individuals, share your own projects, and more, TMC is for you!

Our Community

We offer a platform for our community where anybody can participate on which include forums. Within the forums, users may create threads, blog-like posts, guides, and more!
