What Is TMC?

TMC (The Modding Community) is a community with goals to shine spotlight and support onto the modding community along with make it the norm. We are introducing people to the modding community which in return will improve content creation, gaming, careers, talent, and more!

This is a MAJOR project and has A LOT of potential to change the world with our plans, experience, and ambition! We already have a great team including a game company pushing everything forward and that is only going to expand.


If you want to be a part of a community where you can interact with other talented individuals, share your own projects, and more, TMC is for you!
